### Perceived Likelihood of Achievement: 5/10
- DJ's credentials are world-class (28 years, Deloitte, EY, Shell, 400+ orgs)

### VALUE EQUATION SCORE: (8 x 5) / (3 x 4) = 3.3

**Needs to be 10+ to be a Grand Slam Offer.**

4. **Pre-qualifies for Jumpstart.** Anyone who pays $47 is WARM. The $97/mo upsell on the thank-you page converts 20-30% of $47 buyers.

5. **Recoups ad spend faster.** 30 sales at $47 = $1,410. That nearly covers the entire $1,205 ad investment. THEN the $97/mo becomes pure profit.
