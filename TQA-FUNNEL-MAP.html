The Math — Why This Works

  • 100 paid Career Briefing registrants at $27 = $2,700 (covers ad spend)
  • 50% show rate (paid entry) = 50 attend (vs 12.5% when free)
  • 5% briefing close rate on $997 = 2-3 sales = $2,000-$3,000
  • Follow-up sequence closes another 2-3 in 7 days = $2,000-$3,000
  • Monthly Career Briefing × 12 = $48,000-$72,000/year from briefings alone
  • 20% of $997 students upsell to $4,999-$7,000 CISO Track = $5,000-$14,000/year
  • YouTube + LinkedIn + LinkedIn DM grow organically, reducing ad spend over time
  • Year 1 conservative target: $60,000-$100,000
  • Year 2 with 50K+ YouTube subs: simplify to Boyd's model (free briefing → $2,997)

Key Principles (Proven, Not Made Up)

  • Paid entry filters quality. Boyd can do free because 320K subs self-filter. DJ needs $27 to keep unqualified leads out. (DJ: "Anything that comes free has no value")
  • Pre-call video weeds out tire-kickers. Victoria's insight: if they watch your YouTube and still want to talk, they're serious. A 5-min pre-call video does the same thing at scale.
  • $997 is the sweet spot. Boyd's core offer is $997, not $2,997 (we were wrong). This is serious enough to filter, affordable enough for employed IT pros. Save $4,999-$7,000 for the CISO Track upsell.
  • One funnel, not five. Boyd has ONE path: content → Career Briefing → $997. No quiz, no $47 tripwire, no $97/mo subscription. DJ should simplify the same way.
  • Community is included, not sold. Boyd includes Skool in the $997. No separate $37/mo community. This kills the cannibalization problem DJ flagged.
  • CISO Track is the back-end. Boyd runs Wealth EngineX as a separate back-end offer. DJ's $4,999-$7,000 CISO Track serves the same role for security managers targeting executive roles.
  • Content creates demand, not ads. Boyd's 320K YouTube subs are his real moat. DJ should invest 80% of effort in YouTube + LinkedIn, 20% in ads. Reverse the current ratio.
  • Fear sells, career content converts. Boyd's viral content is scam breakdowns and threat news. His career content is where the money comes from. Use fear to get attention, career proof to get purchases.
  • LinkedIn DM is DJ's unfair advantage. 9K followers, 18 recommendations, and a GHL workflow for outbound DMs to IT Security Managers. No competitor is doing this systematically.
