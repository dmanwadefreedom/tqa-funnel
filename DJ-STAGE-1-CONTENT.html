A viewer watches Video 1 and learns the market exists. Video 2 shows them the money. Video 3 makes them trust DJ. Video 4 creates urgency. Video 5 removes the last doubt. By the time they've watched 3-5 videos, applying for Cybersecurity Accelerator feels like the obvious next step — not a sales pitch.
