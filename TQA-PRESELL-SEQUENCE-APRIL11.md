Up to 80% of cybersecurity roles are filled through referrals, direct outreach, and recruiter pipelines (CIAT/ISC2 data). You're competing for 20% of the available jobs while 80% get filled through connections and conversations you're not part of.

I've hired across four continents. I can tell you exactly how it works from the inside:

1. A role opens. I call people I know first.

By step 4, most roles are filled. The people applying on job boards are competing for what's left.

This is why coaching matters more than another certification. It's not about what you know. It's about who knows you and whether your profile shows up when the right people search.
