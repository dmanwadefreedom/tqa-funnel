Capture + Qualify

Two filters that separate serious people from browsers. Anyone who pays is a real lead.

Filter 1: $47 Career Assessment

Viewers who've watched a few videos see the CTA: "I built a tool that creates your personal career roadmap. $47."

They pay $47 and answer 10-12 questions about their background. Within 24 hours, they get a personalized PDF:

What's in the PDF (8 pages)

PagesContent
1-2Your Score. Where they stand today. Strengths, gaps, what's holding them back.
3-4Your Path. Specific cert order for their background. Sysadmin gets different advice than network engineer.
5-6Your Numbers. Real salary data for current role vs target. Job openings in their metro.
7-8Your 90-Day Plan. Month 1, 2, 3. Which cert, which skills, which roles to target.

The document does NOT sell CISO 360. It gives them a complete plan they could follow alone. One line at the bottom: "If you want help executing this plan, you can apply here."
