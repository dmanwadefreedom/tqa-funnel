Stage 05 Qualification Survey ⏱ Within 24–48hrs post-webinar
Purpose
Self-elimination + warm handoff
  • Serious buyer fills 10 questions — no problem
  • Time-waster drops off at question 3 — good
  • Answers arm the sales team before the call
  • Creates psychological commitment — "I invested time"
Survey Questions
10 qualifying questions
  • Current role + years of experience
  • What's held you back until now?
  • Target salary / current salary
  • Timeline — when do you want to be working in cyber?
  • Have you tried before? What happened?
  • Are you in a position to invest in yourself now?
Lead Scoring
Hot / Warm / Not now
  • 🔥 Hot: IT background + clear timeline + can invest
  • 🌡️ Warm: Right profile, needs timeline nudge
  • ❄️ Not now: No budget or non-technical background
  • Hot leads called within 2 hours of completion

Stage 06 Sales Call ⏱ Within 48–72hrs of survey
Call Structure
45-min discovery + close
  • 0–10 min: Reference their survey answers specifically
  • 10–25 min: Dig into their pain — let them talk
  • 25–35 min: Map their situation to the program outcome
  • 35–45 min: Present investment + handle objections
  • Price revealed only here — never before
Objection Handling
The 3 real objections
  • "Too expensive" → ROI calc — $25K/yr salary jump
  • "No time" → 10–15 hrs/week, working professionals designed
  • "Not technical enough" → 60% of students came from IT, not cyber
  • Payment plan available — never lead with it
Who Takes the Call
Team structure
  • Dedicated closer — not DJ (protects his credibility)
  • DJ joins for last 10 mins on hot leads if needed
  • All calls recorded + reviewed weekly
  • CRM notes updated immediately post-call
