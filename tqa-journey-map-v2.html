Stage 05 · Application
The Self-Qualifying Filter
8-question GHL application filters serious from curious. Only qualified applicants get booked for a strategy call. DJ never sees unqualified leads. The closer reviews applications and books calls within 24hrs. The application itself IS the first sales conversation.

⚙️ Agresh — GHL Form · Closer — Review
Someone who fills out an 8-question application is 4x more likely to close than someone who cold-books a call. The effort signals genuine intent.
The 8 Application Questions
Questions
1. Current role & years of experience
2. Current annual salary (USD/GBP range)
3. Biggest obstacle to moving into cybersecurity?
4. Tried to transition before? What happened?
5. What does success look like in 12 months?
6. Commitment level 1–10 and why?
7. Can you invest 10–15 hrs/week for 14 weeks?
8. How ready are you to invest in your career today?

📞 Closer — Dylan’s network
DJ’s time is protected. He never does these calls. The closer is trained on TQA’s offer, handles objections with clarity not pressure, and knows when to pull back rather than push.
Call Framework (45 min)
0–10 min: Diagnose
"Tell me about where you are right now." Let them talk. Closer listens for pain already in the application. Reflects back what they hear. Never interrupts.
10–25 min: Vision Cast
"What does your life look like if you land that $80K role in 6 months — for your family, your visa situation, your confidence?" They sell themselves.
25–35 min: Reveal the Path
Walk through 14 weeks. What they learn, what support they get. Price comes naturally as part of explaining the program — not as a sudden close moment.
35–45 min: Handle & Decide
Objections: "I need to think" / "Can I afford this?" / "Is now right?" Scripts prepared. Goal: help them make a clear decision, not pressure them into one they’ll regret.
