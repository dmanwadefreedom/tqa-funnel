## SALESLESS MODEL (Taki Moore)
Source: transcript Dylan shared + agent research

- No sales calls. No closers. No DMs.
- Marketing builds desire BEFORE the pitch. Prospect arrives 80%+ convinced.
- Send a Google Doc with exact offer, pricing, timeline, terms.
- Stay silent. Answer questions only. Never close. The offer closes itself.
- For DJ: YouTube + LinkedIn builds trust. Quiz captures lead. Email nurture warms. VSSL presents. Stripe checkout on the page. Done.
- Reference: Taki Moore's $37M approach (Buzzsprout episode 291)

## PODCAST-AS-PROSPECTING (Jordan Lee / Jake Jorgovan play)

**Phase 1: Invite ideal prospects as guests on Cyber Caffeine**
- Target: Information Security Managers, IT Directors who'd buy the $10K CISO program
- Reach out: "I'd love to have you share your story on Cyber Caffeine"

**Phase 2: Interview them about THEIR challenges**
- Topic ties to a pain DJ's program solves
- DJ is the platform, guest owes the relationship

**Phase 3: 48-hour follow-up**
- "Loved our conversation about [specific detail]. I have a program that goes deep on exactly that. Want to explore if it's a fit?"
- Soft. Not a pitch. Natural next step.

**Phase 4: Nurture if no response**
- 2-3 week email sequence with value (templates, frameworks, case studies)
- Then re-engage with offer

**Mix:** 50% prospect guests, 50% authority/name guests for credibility
**Expected conversion:** 5-15% of warm podcast guests at $4,997-$10K
