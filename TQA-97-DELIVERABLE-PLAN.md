## WHAT THE AI ASSISTANT ACTUALLY DOES (not just a chatbot)

It's not "ask ChatGPT about cybersecurity." It's a structured COACH that:

1. **Sends weekly tasks** — every Monday, the member gets "Here's your 3 tasks this week." Not optional. Not suggestions. Tasks.

2. **Reviews their work** — member submits resume draft, AI compares it against cybersecurity job postings and gives specific feedback: "Change 'managed network infrastructure' to 'implemented network security monitoring across 200+ endpoints.'"

3. **Generates personalized content** — interview answers using THEIR background, job target lists for THEIR metro, salary data for THEIR role.

4. **Tracks progress** — "You're 60% through Security+ material. Your practice exam score went from 62% to 78%. You need 5 more days of studying domain 4."

5. **Answers domain-specific questions** — trained on DJ's course content, not the internet. "Should I study for CEH?" → "No. Based on your Network Admin background, CySA+ is a better second cert because it validates threat detection skills you already have."

6. **Holds them accountable** — "You haven't logged in for 5 days. Your Security+ exam is in 3 weeks. Here's a 15-minute task you can do right now to get back on track."
