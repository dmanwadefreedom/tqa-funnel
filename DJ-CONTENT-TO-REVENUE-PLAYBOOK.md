## THE FUNNEL

How someone goes from stranger to paying student. Every step.

### Stage 1: DISCOVERY (they find you)
- They search YouTube for "IT to cybersecurity 2026" and your video shows up
- An IG Reel hits their feed
- A LinkedIn post appears in their timeline
- They hear you on a podcast during their commute
- A current student tells them about you

### Stage 2: TRUST (they watch more)
- They watch 3-5 of your YouTube videos
- They see your case studies. Central Bank policy. The $300K wire fraud. Emirates Nuclear.
- They see student results. Rob G went from chef to $120K analyst. Sunit Suri got placed.
- They think: "This guy actually did it. He's not just teaching theory."

### Stage 3: APPLICATION (they raise their hand)
- They click the link in your YouTube description, IG bio, or LinkedIn
- They fill out the application form (5-8 questions):
  - Current role and years of experience
  - Target role and salary goal
  - Timeline: when do they want to make the switch?
  - Can they invest $5K in their career?
  - How did they find you?
- GHL auto-scores them using the existing lead scoring system (0-12 pts)

### Stage 4: STRATEGY CALL (you qualify them)
- 15 minutes on Zoom. This is not a sales pitch.
- You review their application beforehand.
- You give an honest assessment. "Here's what I'd recommend for your situation."
- If they're a fit: "I'd like to invite you to CISO 360. Here's how it works."
- If they're not ready: "Here's what to do first. Come back when you are."
- The call is about fit. Your credibility does the selling.
